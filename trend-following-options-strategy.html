Strategy Architecture & Rules

This institutional-grade system targets intraday volatility by pairing trend-following filters with price boundary reversal thresholds. Instead of deploying naked directional trades, it harvests option premiums with predefined spreads to restrict tail risk.

  • Trend Filtering: Employs a proprietary trend-following filter to ensure all trade actions align with the macro market direction.
  • Reversal Timing: Enters trade positions precisely when index spot price tests dynamically computed intra-day price boundary levels.
  • Execution Vehicle: Executes defined-risk credit spreads to capture option premium decay while keeping maximum potential loss strictly capped.
  • Dynamic Exits: Automatically closes positions when the spot price breaches dynamic boundaries, or upon triggering internal risk-mitigation rules.
