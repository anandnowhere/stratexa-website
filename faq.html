Frequently Asked Questions

Find clear answers to common technical, operational, and billing questions.

Will I own the source code of my custom bot?

Yes. Unless otherwise agreed upon in writing, all custom-developed strategies are delivered with full source code ownership. You are free to run, modify, or host the code as you wish.

How is strategy confidentiality handled? Do you sign an NDA?

Absolutely. Strategy confidentiality is our highest priority. We sign a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before you share any specific strategy rules, ensuring your intellectual property stays yours.

Which brokers and markets are supported?

We support Indian markets (Zerodha Kite, Dhan, Angel One via APIs), Cryptocurrency (Delta Exchange, Binance, Bybit), and Forex (MT5, Interactive Brokers).

Do you host the bot, or do I need my own server?

We can host it for you via our Monthly Care Plans on secure VPS environments. Alternatively, we can assist you in deploying the code on your own cloud infrastructure (AWS, Azure, or private VPS).

Do you guarantee profits from automated strategies?

No. Stratexa is a software development and technology agency. We write execution systems according to your exact rules. We do not provide financial advice, trading signals, or guarantee strategy performance.

How do you handle API disconnections and rate limits?

Our custom Python engines are engineered with built-in auto-reconnection algorithms, rate-limiting clamps, and error notifications via Slack or Telegram to ensure resilient, round-the-clock trading execution.
